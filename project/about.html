Welcome to SDU Football Association (SFA)!

At SDU, we're not just a university; we're a community of football enthusiasts who share a passion for the beautiful game. The SDU Football Association, also known as SFA, is the beating heart of football culture on our campus.

Who We Are:
SFA is more than just an association; we are a tight-knit community of students united by our love for football. Whether you're a seasoned player, a casual fan, or someone looking to explore the world of football, SFA is the place for you. We celebrate the sport, the camaraderie, and the joy it brings to our lives.

Where we located:


What We Offer:

League Tables and Scoreboards: Stay up-to-date with the latest scores, standings, and statistics from our university football league. Whether you're cheering for a specific team or just love the thrill of competition, our tables and scoreboards have you covered.

Events and Tournaments: Join us for exciting events and tournaments throughout the year. From friendly matches to intense competitions, there's always an opportunity to showcase your skills, make new friends, and, most importantly, have fun!

Football Culture: Immerse yourself in the rich culture of football. Whether it's discussing the latest matches, sharing favorite moments, or debating the greatest players of all time, SFA is the place to connect with like-minded football enthusiasts.
Get Involved: We believe that football is more than just a sport; it's a community. Join us in the celebration of the beautiful game, whether you're a player, a fan, or just curious. SFA is open to everyone who shares a passion for football and wants to be a part of something special.

Explore our website to find out more about upcoming events, join a team, or simply connect with fellow football lovers. Together, let's make the SDU Football Association a vibrant and inclusive hub for all things football at our university.
